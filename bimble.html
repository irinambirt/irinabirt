What is a bimble?

A bimble is a way to pass an hour or so doing a few different things, close by one another and the wonderful journey in between. Not one restaurant or one-place reviews. More of a mooch around.It is simple and fun to create bimbles and explore other peopleâ€™s.
Main idea

Love thy neighbourhood

Bimble is the app for neighbourhoods. It allows users to discover all the things a neighbourhood has to offer - independent shops, delis, restaurants, parks, walks and culture - from people that know those places well.
