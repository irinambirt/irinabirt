For GORGOR I have worked on creating visual designs for the patterns of the 2017 and 2018 fashion collections, as well as promotional materials. A great learning experience was working on the website design and development.

GORGOR It

  • Role - UI& UX designer/ photographer
  • Date - oct 2016- june 2017
The founders of GORGOR were a group of rising generation of artists from around the world who share unique philosophy in life, devotion in art and dedication to fashion. They were gathered originally in an European studio exchanging and collaborating creatively for artistic and design practices. After receiving support from local fashion organisation and group, Regal Glory, the fashion label, GORGOR, was launched in 2015 along with its global presentation.
