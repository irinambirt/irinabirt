TASK

Use Altizure for a immersive projection mapping proposal to showcase the upcoming Qianhai District. Altizure is “the first portal for user generated local 3D maps from drone photographs”- a software that controls DJI drones with the help of DJI SDK to capture vertical and oblique photographs.

IDEA

In order to create an immersive environment that best showcases and amplifies the realistic quality of our 3D animation, while allowing the audience to experience Qianhai District from the view of a pedestrian, as if walking down the streets surrounded by grand modern architectures, we propose to construct a cubic room for display.

RESULT

The result is a setting for audiences to travel in shifted space and time, arriving at the vibrant coastal Qianhai District in the year of 2020.

Projection mapping an environment model

For this we used VPT which is a projection mapping software that turns objects into a display surface for video projection.
